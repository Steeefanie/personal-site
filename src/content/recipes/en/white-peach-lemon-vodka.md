## Ingredients

- 30 ml ABSOLUT vodka
- 100 ml Genki Forest white-peach sparkling water
- 100 ml lemon-flavored vitamin C drink
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Fill a glass with ice, then add the vodka and lemon drink.
2. Stir until thoroughly chilled.
3. Add the white-peach sparkling water and lift once or twice to combine.
