## Ingredients

- 5 ml ABSOLUT Citron vodka
- 100 ml RIO Blue Rose whisky-flavored premix
- 100 ml original Yakult
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add ice, lemon vodka, and Yakult to a glass and stir gently.
2. Add the Blue Rose premix and lift once or twice to combine.
