## Ingredients

- 300 g minced meat;
- ½–1 onion, sliced;
- 4–5 garlic cloves, minced;
- 1–2 potatoes, cut into chunks;
- about 80 ml uncooked rice by volume;
- whole milk, as needed;
- curry roux blocks, ground black pepper, butter, chilli powder, and light soy sauce, as needed.

## Method

1. Place the potatoes in cold water, bring to a boil, then simmer until just tender but not falling apart. Drain well.
2. Cook the rice at the same time and set it aside.
3. Heat a little oil in a frying pan and brown the potato pieces. Remove and reserve.
4. Cook the minced meat over medium heat, breaking it up and allowing all released liquid to evaporate.
5. Add the garlic and black pepper, toss briefly, then remove the meat from the pan.
6. Melt the butter in the same pan. Add the onion and cook until soft and golden at the edges.
7. Return the meat, add the curry roux, and stir until it melts and coats the meat evenly.
8. Add chilli powder and light soy sauce and mix well.
9. Pour in enough whole milk to form a sauce, add the potatoes, and simmer over low heat until thickened.
10. Fold in the cooked rice and serve once evenly combined.
