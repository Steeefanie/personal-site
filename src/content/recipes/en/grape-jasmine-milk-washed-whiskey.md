## Ingredients

- 40 ml Jim Beam bourbon whiskey
- 120 ml Bright Dairy whole milk
- 40 ml Wei-Chuan NFC grape juice
- 40 ml Oriental Leaf jasmine tea
- About 10 ml fresh lemon juice, roughly one quarter of a lemon
- 2 teaspoons white sugar, about 8 g
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Stir the bourbon, grape juice, jasmine tea, lemon juice, and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
2. Put the milk in a separate container, then slowly pour the mixed drink into the milk and stir once.
3. Strain through a fine sieve, then slowly through a coffee filter. Return the first cloudy runnings to the filter if needed.
4. Pour the clarified drink into a glass over ice.
