## Ingredients

- 30 ml Malibu coconut liqueur
- 10 ml Havana Club 3 Años white rum
- 60 ml Fentel pineapple juice
- 60 ml Coconut Palm coconut drink
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add all liquid ingredients to a shaker and fill with ice.
2. Shake hard for about 12 seconds.
3. Strain into a glass over fresh ice.
