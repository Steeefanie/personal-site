## Ingredients

- 60 ml Ai Wenshan bourbon whiskey
- 30 ml fresh lemon juice
- 10 ml simple syrup
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add the bourbon, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a shaker.
2. Fill with ice and shake hard for 10–12 seconds.
3. Strain into a glass over fresh ice.
