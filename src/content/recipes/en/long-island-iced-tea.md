## Ingredients

- 20 ml Soviet Red vodka
- 20 ml Olmeca Silver tequila
- 20 ml Hayman’s gin
- 20 ml Havana Club 3 Años white rum
- 20 ml Cointreau
- 30 ml fresh lemon juice
- 20 ml simple syrup
- 60–90 ml Coca-Cola
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add every liquid except the cola to a shaker and fill with ice.
2. Shake for about 10 seconds and strain into a glass over fresh ice.
3. Top with cola and lift once to combine.
