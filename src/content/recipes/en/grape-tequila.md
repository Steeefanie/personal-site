## Ingredients

- 40 ml Olmeca Silver tequila
- 60 ml Wei-Chuan grape juice
- 100 ml original Yakult
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add ice and tequila to a glass and stir to chill.
2. Add the grape juice and Yakult, then stir until evenly combined.
