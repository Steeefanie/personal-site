## Ingredients

- 15 ml ABSOLUT Citron vodka
- 105 ml original Yakult
- 105 ml Genki Forest white-peach sparkling water
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add ice and lemon vodka to a glass.
2. Add the Yakult and stir gently.
3. Pour in the white-peach sparkling water and lift once or twice to combine.
