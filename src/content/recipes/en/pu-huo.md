## Ingredients

- 15 ml Soviet Red vodka
- 15 ml Hayman’s gin
- 30 ml NFC grape juice
- 140 ml lemon- or grapefruit-flavored vitamin C drink
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add the vodka, gin, and grape juice to a shaker and shake with ice for about 10 seconds.
2. Add fresh ice to a glass, then pour in the vitamin drink first.
3. Slowly pour the strained mixture over the back of a bar spoon so it floats above the vitamin drink and preserves the layers.
