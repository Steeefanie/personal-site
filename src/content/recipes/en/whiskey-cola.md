## Ingredients

- 40 ml Ai Wenshan bourbon whiskey
- 10 ml fresh lemon juice
- 160 ml Coca-Cola
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Fill a glass with ice, then add the bourbon and lemon juice.
2. Stir until the glass is chilled.
3. Pour the cola down the side of the glass and lift once to combine.
