## Ingredients

- 30 ml Malibu coconut liqueur
- 30 ml Fentel pineapple juice
- 30 ml Coconut Palm coconut drink
- 30 ml original Yakult
- Ice as needed

## Method

1. Add ice and all liquid ingredients to a glass.
2. Stir until evenly combined and thoroughly chilled.
